About Dennie's

Run by Keiron for over five years, Dennie's is Cowfold's local hangout — daily regulars, friendly faces, and a warm welcome whether you're from the village or just passing through. All are welcome and we try to accommodate any and all requests.

Everything made fresh to order. Eat in or take away.
